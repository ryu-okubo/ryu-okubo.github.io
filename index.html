    Research Project

    Brain Computer Interface in psychiatry

    Our team specializes in the development of a cutting-edge Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) application within the realm of clinical psychiatry. Through the integration of a state-of-the-art BCI system with Virtual Reality (VR) technology, our pioneering project aims to detect real-time brain signals from subjects, enabling the alteration of the VR environment based on the extracted information. With a primary focus on treating height phobia-related conditions, our innovative system demonstrates tremendous potential. Additionally, it offers researchers a safe and cost-effective environment for testing BCI-related medical systems.
